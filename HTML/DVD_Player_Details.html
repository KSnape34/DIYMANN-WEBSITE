Product Information

Access all the best content and watch in stunning high definition with the BP350 Smart Blu-ray & DVD Player with instant streaming and built-in WiFi.

Good To Know

  • Get the most from your HDTV – the BP350 plays all your favourites in exceptional Full HD 1080p, enhanced with immersive high-definition sound.
  • There's no need to replace your DVD collection either. The BP350 upscales your old DVDs to impressive image qualities thanks to 1080p upscaling via HDMI. Re-watch all the classics in superb detail, like never before.
  • Access premium content and stream your favourite films and shows from providers like Netflix and YouTube.
  • Connect your Blu-ray player to the internet and enjoy a world of entertainment at your fingertips. Set up is simple with an existing wireless broadband network and you don't have to worry about wires.
  • Share audio, videos and photos across your home network. The LG BP350 works with any other DLNA-certified devices to complete your at home entertainment system.
  • The BP350 is compatible with LG MusicFlow, so you can listen and control all of your content through the Music Flow Player app.
  • Simply connect your LG Music Flow speaker to your network via WiFi and access music through your LG Blu-ray player.
  • What's more, thanks to multi-room functionality you can play CD content to multiroom speakers for all-round sound.
